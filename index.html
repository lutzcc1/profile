Plan-et

This application is for travelers to be able to create their trips and share them with the community. New travelers can take a look at different travel plans created by other users to get some inspiration while creating theirs.

Developed in Rails, this app relies heavily on Javascript and some Ajax to create and modify the activities inside the trips. Most of the features this app has were implemented with concepts I didn't know back then. It was a great learning experience.

Hom-et

This is a web application to share experiences and great moments between locals and tourist. The excuse? A delicious homemade meal.

Foreign tourists can book meals at locals households so that they can enjoy homemade local food while meeting new and exciting people.

On the other side, locals can receive foreign tourists so that they can share their traditions and local expertise while making new friends.
